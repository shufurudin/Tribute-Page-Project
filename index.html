Asashoryu

The Yokozuna

Asashoryu Akinori is his rikishi name and he is natural from Mongolia. He was one the most successful rikishi in the hole sumo history, being the first Mongolian to rise at the highest rank of sumo (Yokozuna) and the first rikishi to win all 6 bashos in a row!



But he is more like a anti-hero than a hero. Asashoryu was envolve in a lot of miss conducts, in the dohyo, for example, he was the first Yokozuna to receive a hansoku-make in a bout and out the dohyo unfortunately he made several bad actions from be seen without dressing the typical kimono until aggressions and drunking.
